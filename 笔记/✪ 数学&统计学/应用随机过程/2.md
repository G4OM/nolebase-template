$$
①M^{n}_X(t) = \mathbb{E}\left(X^ne^{tX}\right) = \int_{-\infty}^{\infty} x^ne^{tx} f_X(x) \, dx
$$
$$
t=0时，原点矩： M^{n}_X(0) = \int_{-\infty}^{\infty} x^n f_X(x) \, dx
$$
-- -- 
$$
M_X(t) = \mathbb{E}[e^{tX}] = \sum_{x} e^{tx} p(x)
$$
